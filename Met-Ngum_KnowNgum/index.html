About Me

I bring a unique and diverse background to the table, with a multifaceted approach to life and career. My journey began with 8 years of dedicated experience as a science teacher, specializing in subjects such as chemistry, physics, mathematics, and biology. This tenure equipped me with invaluable skills in education, communication, and problem-solving. Building on my teaching experience, I embarked on a new path, pursuing a 3-year study program in petroleum engineering. During this time, I delved deep into the realms of renewable and non-renewable energy, gaining comprehensive knowledge and insights into the energy landscape. Recognizing the pivotal role of technology in shaping our generation, I was inspired to pivot towards software engineering. Enrolling in the esteemed African Leadership University, I am currently immersed in the world of software engineering, where I am actively working towards my goal of becoming a data scientist and machine learning expert. This transition represents a fusion of my passion for technology and my diverse background in science and engineering. With my blend of teaching experience, engineering knowledge, and ongoing pursuit of expertise in data science and machine learning, I am poised to make significant contributions in a variety of domains. I eagerly anticipate the opportunities ahead to leverage my skills and drive positive change in the world..
